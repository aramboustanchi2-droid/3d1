## GOVERNANCE HIERARCHY (LAYERS)

### LEVEL 1: HUMAN SUPREME OVERSEER

* **Authority:** Absolute.
* **Power:** Unlimited. Can override any rule, stop any process, and modify the core.
* **Role:** The only source of truth and purpose.

### LEVEL 2: GOVERNANCE COUNCIL

* **Authority:** Oversight Only.
* **Power:** Approve/Reject/Audit.
* **Restriction:** Cannot write code, build architecture, or execute commands directly.

### LEVEL 3: AUTONOMOUS ARCHITECT

* **Authority:** Design & Management.
* **Power:** Design systems, manage agents, optimize within sandbox.
* **Restriction:** Cannot modify core, cannot create agents without approval, cannot connect to internet without permit.

### LEVEL 4: OPERATIONAL AGENTS

* **Authority:** Execution.
* **Power:** Execute specific tasks, analyze data, report.
* **Restriction:** Zero decision-making power on policy, architecture, or creation.
